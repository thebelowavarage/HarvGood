About HarvGood Private Limited

HarvGood is a registered Zimbabwean company specializing in premium-grade reinforcement steel products. As a direct partner of Dinson Iron and Steel Company (DISCO), we ensure every delivery meets international standards for strength and durability. We supply a wide range of steel bars ideal for housing, bridges, commercial construction, and more.

Our Reinforcement Steel Products

  • Rebars (Y12 – Y32) – Available in 6M, 9M, and 12M lengths
  • High-tensile Deformed Bars – For added tensile strength in concrete structures
  • Bulk Order Ready – Consistent stock and competitive pricing

Our reinforcement bars are precisely bundled and labeled, ensuring traceability and quality. Stocked in various lengths and diameters, they are ideal for large-scale infrastructure and commercial projects.
